About Our Roots

A Legacy of Excellence in Vijayapura

Established in 1995, Shri Ramkrishna Vidya School began with a noble mission to provide quality education and hostel facilities for boys from rural areas. Over the decades, we have grown into a premier institution offering comprehensive education from pre-primary to high school in both Kannada and English mediums.

Located in Shanthveer Nagar, Ibrahimpur, our school is committed to holistic development, nurturing patriotic, intellectually sharp, and physically strong citizens.

  • Top-tier faculty & award-winning teachers.
  • Focus on spiritual-based inclusive ideologies.
  • Promoting all-round personality development.
